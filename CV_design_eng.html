about:

I define myself as a multidisciplinary designer who likes to tackle each commission from a broad and collaborative perspective. I think of commissions as opportunities to discover people, stories and interests to promote and explain through design and the product to achieve in particular. I have experience designing the website and merchandising of the association ASAV (asav.tv), as well as my personal website where I have some of the commissions I have done (manuellatour.cat).
